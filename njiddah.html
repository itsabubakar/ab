Dr. Abdulkadeer Babangida Njiddah, is a PhD holder of Accounting and Finance, born on 29th December, 1965 in Song, Adamawa State Nigeria. He is an astute Accountant, financial expert with proven long-standing integrity, a gentleman per excellence who has gained experiences in leaps and bounds in the services to humanity and his nation generally.

Mr. Abdulkadeer Babangida Njiddah received requisite educational qualifications that distinguished him among equals of which, he obtained his undergraduate degree in Accounting – B.Sc. Hon (1989) from University of Maiduguri and further proceeded for his Masters in Accounting and Finance at Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria were he obtained M.Sc in Accounting and Finance in 2013. Mr. Abdulkadeer Babangida Njiddah received his PhD in Accounting and Finance from Ahmadu bello University Zaria in 2017.

In his quest and pursuit for an accounting career he developed his professional skills in the field of finance, accounting, audit, taxation, and advisory services and had professional certifications. Mr. Abdulkadeer Babangida Njiddah is a fellow with Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN) in 2013 and Institute of Internal Auditors of Nigeria –(FIIA) in 2005. Also, an associate member with the Institute of Chartered Taxation (CITN) in 2002, Institute of Directors in 2018 and has registered with Financial Reporting Council of Nigeria accounting regulatory body. In addition to consultancy and advisory role, he has skills for developing and managing client relationships, identifying new business opportunities and contributing to growth and development. These all go to prove his insatiable appetite for knowledge and quest for human capital development.

Career Experiences
He is a fellow member of Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria with over 30 years standing in the profession. He has wide experience in the field of Finance, Audit & Investigation, Assurance, Accounting and consultancy services and has been involved in offering these services to both public and private sectors. He has served in various capacities in both public and private sector where he rise to the position of Director/Executive Director. He is the Principal Partner of Abdulkadeer & Co., a firm of chartered accountants. He also serves as a member of various reconciliation committees on recovery and revenue enhancement to all States Government and private sector. Dr Abdulkadeer Njiddah currently serve as the Chairman Statutory Audit Committee of TAJ Bank Plc, Nigeria.
