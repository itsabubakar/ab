Ibiyeye Busayo Oyewole is an accomplished professional with a strong background in audit and assurance. He holds a Master's in Business Administration from Ahmadu Bello University and a Bachelor's degree in Accounting from the same institution. Busayo is a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (FCA - ICAN) and an Associate Chartered of the Institute of Taxation (ACTI â€“ CITN). He is also registered with the Financial Reporting Council (FRC/2018/ICAN/00000018674).

His educational journey includes an IJMB (Advance Level) from Kwara State Polytechnic and secondary school education at Oro Grammar School in Kwara State. He began his professional journey as a classroom teacher during his National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) at Government Secondary School, Zagga, Kebbi State.

Busayo's career at Abdulkadeer & CO. started as an Audit Senior from 2008 to 2011, while he conducted audits and investigations for both public and private organizations. He also provided accounting and financial management services, undertook tax management, and handled receivership and receiver management services for the firm. He played a crucial role in designing and installing accounting manuals for numerous clients.

Promoted to Assistant Manager from July 2011 to 2015 at Abdulkadeer & CO. (Audit and Consulting), Busayo continued his responsibilities in audit, accounting, and tax services. He demonstrated proficiency in implementing efficient financial management consultancy services and designing operational manuals. Notably, he played a key role in verifying and implementing value-for-money audits, especially concerning the allocation, disbursement, and utilization of funds by the Tertiary Education Trust Funds to institutions in Nigeria.

Busayo has furthest enhanced his professional expertise through various training programs, including the Technology Competency Initiative (TCI) by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria and the ICAN Mandatory Continuing Professional Education on Tax Practice, Consultancy, Audit, and Accountancy. He has also completed the ICAN IFRS Certification Training Programme Modules I and II.

In summary, Ibiyeye Busayo Busayo's extensive experience in audit, financial management, and consultancy, coupled with his educational and professional achievements, positions him as a highly capable Managing Partner specializing in Audit & Assurance.
