Abubakar Ali is an accomplished professional with extensive experience in business development, audit, and financial consulting. He holds a Master's degree in Procurement and Supply Chain Management from Kaduna State University, complemented by a Postgraduate Diploma in the same field. Additionally, he obtained a Bachelor's degree in Accountancy from the University of Maiduguri.

Abubakar is a member of the Chartered Institute of Purchasing and Supply and has undergone specialized training in Business Development and Relationship Management, as well as Successful Negotiation strategies.

With a notable career spanning over 17 years in the banking industry, Abubakar worked at Afribank Nigeria Plc from 1990 to 2007, while he rose from a Graduate Assistant to the position of Branch Manager. His roles included Business Development Manager, Service Delivery Manager, Head of Cash, Head of Clearing, Head of Forex, and Credit Officer, showcasing a diverse skill set.

From 2008 to 2018, Abubakar served as the Assistant Audit Manager at Abdulkadeer & CO., whise he led audit and financial consulting initiatives. His responsibilities included audit and investigation of accounts, accounting and financial management services, tax management, and the design and implementation of accounting manuals.

Currently, as the Business Development Partner at Abdulkadeer & Co. since 2018, Abubakar plays a pivotal role in driving business growth through innovation and client service improvement. His duties encompass setting goals, developing business and revenue growth plans, maintaining client relationships, and identifying new business opportunities. He actively collaborates with professionals and design teams, research industry trends, and trains business development staff to meet revenue goals.

Abubakar's notable achievements include his significant rise in the banking sector and his active participation in various audit and tax consultancies for private, parastatals, government agencies, recruitment representatives, and corporate finance management consultancy. His comprehensive experience underscores his capability to handle diverse business development assignments.
